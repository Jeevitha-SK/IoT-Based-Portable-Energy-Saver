# IoT-Based Portable Energy Saver

## 📌 Project Overview

The IoT-Based Portable Energy Saver is a smart energy management system designed to monitor and control electrical appliance usage. It helps reduce unnecessary power consumption by automatically detecting idle devices and switching them off, thereby improving overall energy efficiency.

## 🎯 Objective

The main objective of this project is to minimize energy wastage in homes and workplaces by implementing an automated and intelligent power monitoring system.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* Internet of Things (IoT)
* Embedded System Components
* Sensors for Power Monitoring
* Microcontroller (Arduino / NodeMCU)
* Automation Control Mechanism

## ⚙️ System Features

* Real-time monitoring of appliance energy usage
* Automatic detection of idle devices
* Auto power cutoff to prevent energy wastage
* Portable and easy-to-install design
* Improves energy efficiency and reduces electricity cost

## 📊 Working Principle

The system continuously monitors the power consumption of connected appliances. When an appliance remains idle for a specific period, the system automatically cuts off power supply to prevent unnecessary energy usage.

## 🌍 Applications

* Smart homes
* Offices
* Hostels and dormitories
* Industrial energy management

## 🎯 Benefits

* Reduces electricity bills
* Prevents energy wastage
* Supports sustainable energy usage
* Enhances automation and convenience
